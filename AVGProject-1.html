AVG Project

At the HTM office we use an application to handle and manage official reports (proces-verbally). Additionally, this application has multiple functions that I won't mention here. The task, in broad terms, was to apply GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation) to this system. Within this task, it includes anonymizing official reports in the database that are older than 5 years. Furthermore, documents linked to a report should be deleted.
